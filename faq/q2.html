Q2. Can I close my eyes when meditating?

Beginners should not close their eyes during meditation, as they may not yet be familiar with maintaining proper posture and body awareness.

After several months of regular practice, once you're more accustomed to the meditation technique, you can begin closing your eyes to enter a deeper meditative state. However, whenever you feel tired, sleepy,..., it's recommended to keep your eyes open to stay aware of your body and avoid falling asleep.
